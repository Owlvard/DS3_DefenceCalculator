  let's now calculate how much actual damage there attack is reduced.                          
  As an example if I have 30 Defence absorption, and someone attacks me with 200AR that should result in:                        
                             
  200AR * 30% = 60 AR in other words: I take 60 Damage les.                           
                             
  Formula: Incomming Attack * Defence absorption = absolute damage reduced
